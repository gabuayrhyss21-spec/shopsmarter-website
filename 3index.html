About SmartShop

This site is designed to help online shoppers make smarter decisions by addressing common problems in e-commerce. Many consumers struggle with misleading discounts, confusing product sizes, and a lack of price transparency. By applying mathematical concepts such as percentages, unit pricing, value comparison, and quality-to-price ratio, this site ensures that buyers can clearly see the true cost of products and make rational, informed choices.
